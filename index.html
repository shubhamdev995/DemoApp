FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our credit program before applying.

Who is eligible to apply for a fuel credit account?
Any registered business — transport company, contractor, school, logistics firm, agricultural cooperative — with valid business documents can apply. Individuals operating commercial vehicles may also qualify based on consumption volume.
What documents are required for the credit account application?
You’ll need: GST Registration Certificate, PAN Card (business/individual), Cancelled Cheque, Vehicle Registration Copies, and a signed Credit Agreement form. Our team will guide you through the process end-to-end.
How long does credit account approval take?
Once all documents are submitted, our verification process is completed within 24–48 working hours. You’ll receive confirmation via WhatsApp and your account will be active immediately.
Can I increase my credit limit once the account is active?
Yes. After maintaining 3 months of consistent payment history, you can request a credit limit review. Limits are enhanced based on your consumption history and payment track record.
How are billing statements delivered?
Statements are sent via WhatsApp and email on the 1st of every month. You can also request transaction details anytime by contacting your dedicated account manager.
Is there a security deposit required?
For Silver accounts, a refundable security deposit equivalent to one billing cycle’s expected consumption may be required. Gold and Platinum account deposit requirements are discussed during the approval process.
Can multiple vehicles and drivers use the same account?
Absolutely. Fleet accounts support unlimited vehicles and drivers under a single credit umbrella. Each driver/vehicle is assigned a unique tracking code and you get consolidated monthly reporting across your entire fleet.
